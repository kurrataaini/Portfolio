About Me

Data Analyst & Data Scientist

I am a fresh graduate in Informatics Engineering from Universitas Maritim Raja Ali Haji. Starting from my 5th semester , I actively joined the Kampus Merdeka programs, beginning with a research internship at BRIN , where I was part of the Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity Research Center based in Bandung. During this onsite program, I conducted research in machine learning using clustering methods to identify potential drug candidates for COVID-19 based on abstracts from numerous scientific articles, which resulted in a scientific paper that is currently being prepared for publication.

In my 6th semester , I participated in the Magang dan Studi Independen Bersertifikat (MSIB) program at the Red Hat IBM Academy for Hybrid Cloud and AI in Infinite learning, Batam. There, I studied topics including the use of Red Hat OpenShift and IBM Cloud, the fundamentals of AI and ML, IBM tools such as Watson AI for NLP and computer vision, data preparation and model training, as well as cloud security with hands-on practice using both Red Hat and IBM tools. As a result, I earned the RHCSA certification and developed a chatbot using IBM products as my capstone project.

In the 7th semester, I continued in the same program as a mentor, guiding around 20 mentees through their learning process, certification preparation, and capstone projects. I also completed my undergraduate thesis on deep learning and computer vision, specifically using the ResNet algorithm for image classification to identify fish freshness.
